About the Job

Position: Business Development Manager - Staffing

Duration: Fulltime-Permanent

Location: Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh or Hyderabad, Telangana

• If you are seeking to achieve rapid growth with the assistance of a dependable delivery team and the most trustworthy, rapidly expanding company (which has doubled in size over the last two years), and desire to join a management team that is committed to a vision, then this opportunity is tailored for you!.


• Regardless of your location, you are welcome to apply. However, if you happen to be located in Tirupati, Andhra Pradesh or Hyderabad, India you will have the added benefit of being surrounded by incredible team


• Build strong relationships Alcheminds Solutions offers best customer service experience, automate sales and marketing, improve operations, we’re the nextgen solution for you. Our expert team works with you to create and evolve a unique AI assistant, to automate improve and enhance your customer experience.


Responsibilities:

• You will have direct revenue responsibility for building and managing an annual revenue pipeline.

• This is a quota-based position, with responsibility for meeting quarterly and annual targets

• Responsible for the profitability of assigned accounts (revenue and margins)

• Responsible for closing new logos, direct clients, and implementation partners

• Analyze clients’ requirements and establish a plan for placement

• Provide customer service and counsel clients on the strategy to attract onboard talent


Qualifications:

• 8+ years prior IT staffing/consulting/business development experience building staffing accounts

• Experience successfully selling IT Staffing Services to meet quarterly and annual revenue targets.

• Excellent communication skills (verbal and written)

• Ability to manage multiple clients across the US market.

• The high degree of accountability and drive

• Prior Experience in staffing and/or recruiting is a must-have

• Experience with an ATS is a plus


Education:

• Bachelor's Degree required , Master's Degree / MBA preferred
